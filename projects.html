Our Projects

We build practical digital products that create measurable impact. Below are two flagship projects that reflect our blend of domain expertise, modern engineering, and long-term partnership mindset.

Cacao-Tech — Farmer-First Traceability & Data

Cacao-Tech is a farmer-first, FAIR-principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) digital platform that brings transparency to the cacao value chain in Ecuador. Hyves.Tech designed and developed the core IT solution: farmer registration, plot mapping, harvest/quality capture, and secure data flows that feed analytics and Power BI dashboards.

The system supports quality assessment (incl. NIR data capture), whole-pod processing pilots, and end-to-end traceability that helps stakeholders prepare for EU deforestation compliance and unlock value from pulp and husk side streams. Built for scalability and open collaboration with supply-chain partners.

Equus — BTW Vakantiewoningen Workflow

For Equus (accountants & tax advisors), we engineered the backend and app logic that supports their BTW vakantiewoningen proposition: onboarding property owners as VAT entrepreneurs, guiding data capture for invoicing and quarterly VAT returns, and streamlining secure client communication.

The solution centralizes user management (Keycloak/Azure AD integration), roles & permissions, and audit-ready data storage—reducing administrative friction while improving compliance and client experience.
