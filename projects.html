Lime · Vietnam · Live

Citrus-Tech

Smart field robot for lime farming in Long An

An autonomous field robot, IoT soil sensors, and a farm dashboard built specifically for canal-based lime orchards in Bến Lức. Computer vision detects pests and disease in real time, while the platform turns peel waste into traceable cold-pressed oil. Built in partnership with RMIT University Vietnam capstone teams.

What's Next

Where do we go next?

Coffee, mango, coconut — the next crops on the roadmap. Looking for cooperatives, buyers, and partners in the right markets.

☕
Ethiopia · Vietnam
Coffee
Cherry pulp valorization (cascara, coffee flour) for one of the world's largest smallholder crops. Ethiopia and Vietnam both have massive smallholder bases ready for traceability infrastructure.
🥭
West Africa · SE Asia
Mango
Peel and seed kernel produce significant oil and fiber value. High EU import volumes and growing pressure for traceable, sustainable mango supply chains.
🥥
Indonesia · Philippines
Coconut
Multiple valorization streams — shell, husk, water — and an enormous smallholder base across Southeast Asia. The coconut chain is structurally similar to the cacao chain we already serve.
