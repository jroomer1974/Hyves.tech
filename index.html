How We Work

One model. Any crop.
Any emerging market.

A repeatable stack that turns isolated smallholder farmers into connected, traceable, circular producers — without requiring them to become technology experts.

01
See the Farm
Field robots and IoT sensors replace hours of daily manual walking. Early detection. Real data. No more guesswork.
02
Know the Chain
Every harvest, spray decision, and soil reading captured in our platform — a traceable record from field to buyer.
03
Open the Market
Traceability data enables EUDR compliance, GLOBALG.A.P., and premium pricing isolated farmers cannot access alone.
04
Close the Loop
Peel, pulp, husk — what the farmer discards becomes a second income as traceable oils, fibers, and ingredients.
What We Solve

First-mile problems
that block real impact

Smallholder farmers in emerging markets face the same structural gaps everywhere: no visibility, no traceability, and no second income from what they waste. We close all three.

Compliance

EUDR Compliance Made Easy

The EU Deforestation Regulation requires verified, traceable supply chains for cocoa, coffee, palm oil, and more. We give smallholder cooperatives the data infrastructure to comply — and to keep their European market access.

Learn More →
Sustainability

Circular Income from Waste Streams

A single farmer's lime peel is waste. A network of 100 traceable farmers' peel is a product. We build the infrastructure that turns crop side-streams into certified ingredients — and a second income for the farmer.

Our Platform

Tech That Makes
a Difference

We turn data into tools — which means more visibility for farmers, more compliance for buyers, and more value extracted from every part of the crop.

Field Robotics & IoT

  • Autonomous robots for canal-based orchards
  • Computer vision pest & disease detection
  • Soil sensors: pH, NPK, moisture, EC
  • Built with RMIT Vietnam engineering teams

Traceability Platform

  • Farm-level traceability plot to export
  • Power BI dashboards for all stakeholders
  • ERP, lab, and logistics integration
  • Offline-capable for low-connectivity farms

Compliance & Markets

  • EUDR deforestation compliance ready
  • GLOBALG.A.P. & food safety supported
  • Sustainability reporting for EU buyers
  • Cooperative aggregation for collective impact

Circular Valorization

  • Cold-pressed lime oil & D-Limonene pathways
  • Cacao pulp & husk for food ingredients
  • Traceability makes waste streams premium
  • Direct lines to European ingredient buyers
