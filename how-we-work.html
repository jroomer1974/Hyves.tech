Our Model

One model.
Any crop.
Any emerging market.

A repeatable stack that turns isolated smallholder farmers into connected, traceable, circular producers — without requiring them to become technology experts.

01
See the Farm
From manual walking
to continuous data.

A farmer walking a 1-hectare lime orchard spends 5–6 hours a day just observing. We replace that with field robots and IoT sensors that generate continuous, structured data — so the farmer sees problems earlier and acts smarter.

Autonomous robots
Designed for canal-based and narrow-plot orchards in Long An.
Computer vision
Real-time pest, disease, and weed coverage detection.
Soil sensors
pH, moisture, NPK, and electrical conductivity captured continuously.
Zero manual logging
Data flows directly to the platform — the farmer just walks the field.
Field Robotics IoT Sensors Computer Vision ROS 2
02
Know the Chain
Every harvest.
Every spray.
Every plot.

Every harvest record, spray decision, soil reading, and quality assessment is captured in our platform — a living, auditable record of what happens on the farm. This data is the foundation for everything that follows.

Plot-to-export traceability
Farm-level data follows every kilo of fruit through the chain.
Real-time dashboards
Power BI views for farmers, co-ops, buyers, and certification bodies.
System integration
Connects with ERP, quality labs, and logistics systems.
Offline-capable
Works in farms with limited or intermittent connectivity.
Traceability Platform Power BI Data Integration
03
Open the Market
Compliance becomes
a competitive advantage.

An isolated farmer cannot prove his fruit is safe, sustainable, or traceable. A connected farmer — with a data record behind him — can. Our platform turns compliance from a burden into a competitive advantage, opening export markets that were previously inaccessible.

EUDR-ready
Deforestation compliance data exportable on demand.
GLOBALG.A.P. supported
Food safety and good practice documentation built in.
Sustainability reporting
For EU buyers, retailers, and regulators.
Cooperative aggregation
Individual farms, collective credentials, shared market access.
EUDR Compliance GLOBALG.A.P. Export Access
04
Close the Loop
Waste becomes
a second income.

What the farmer discards after harvest — peel, pulp, husk, seed — has real value. But only if it's traceable. Our platform generates the data that makes waste streams into certified, marketable ingredients: cold-pressed citrus oil, cacao fiber, coffee flour. A second income built on data the farmer already has.

Citrus peel valorization
Cold-pressed lime oil, D-Limonene, and dried fiber pathways.
Cacao side streams
Pulp and husk turned into food ingredients and fiber.
Premium positioning
Traceability data turns commodity waste into certified ingredients.
Direct buyer connection
Linked to European ingredient buyers via Wageningen Food Valley.
Circular Economy Waste Valorization Ingredient Markets
Why This Works

Designed for the farmer.
Built to scale.

Farmer-first design
Technology that doesn't improve the farmer's life is not worth building. Every decision starts with what the farmer needs, not what's technically interesting.
The network effect
One farmer's data is useful. One hundred farmers' data is powerful. Waste streams only become viable at collective scale. The hive works because the bees share.
Repeatable by design
The platform and model are crop-agnostic. Built for cacao in Ecuador, adapted for lime in Vietnam. The next crop takes weeks, not years.
Rooted in Wageningen
Based in Wageningen Food Valley, in close collaboration with WUR. We understand what European buyers, regulators, and funders need — and deliver it through teams who understand the farmer.
